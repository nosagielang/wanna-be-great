Breakfast Food

I make this breakfast whenever I'm hungry between the hours of 8 a.m. and 12 p.m. It's usually very filling and unhealthy which is everything I look for in a meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Thick cut bacon
  • Grits
  • Butter
  • 2 Pillsbury Honey Butter Biscuits
  • Salt
  • Pepper

What To Do

  • Set oven to 350F. Lay biscuits on nonstick pan. Put in oven for 15 minutes.
  • Add water to grits and put on the stove. Let cook until it's thick and not watery. Add butter, salt, and pepper.
  • Heat skillet and add the bacon. Fry bacon until crispy. Make sure to cook on both sides
  • Heat skillet. Whip eggs and add salt and peper. Pour eggs into skillet and cook until desired consistency.
  • Put all cooked foods on a plate. Enjoy your meal and all its unhealthy possibilities./li>
