What are we making?

by Nosagie Lang

I make this breakfast whenever I'm hungry between the hours of 8 a.m. and 12 p.m. It's usually very filling and unhealthy which is everything I look for in a meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Thick cut bacon
  • Grits
  • Butter
  • 2 Pillsbury Honey Butter Biscuits
  • Salt
  • Pepper

What to do

  1. Set oven to 350F. Lay biscuits on nonstick pan. Put in oven for 15 minutes.
  2. Add water to grits and put on the stove. Let cook until it's thick and not watery. Add butter, salt, and pepper.
  3. Heat skillet and add the bacon. Fry bacon until crispy. Make sure to cook on both sides
  4. Heat skillet. Whip eggs and add salt and peper. Pour eggs into skillet and cook until desired consistency.
  5. Put all cooked foods on a plate. Enjoy your meal and all its unhealthy possibilities.
